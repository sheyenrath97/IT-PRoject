Welcome

Lucky cats have been a part of my life since early adulthood. I found them through anime, and over time, they became more than symbols—they became friends. Their presence brings me peace, luck, and comfort.

My First Lucky Cat

My first lucky cat was a black cookie jar from World Market. It wasn't just a decoration—it felt like a part of me. When my ex-husband broke it, it felt like a piece of my heart cracked too. Even now, I remember how it made me feel: safe, understood, and never alone.

Why I Love Them

  • Cats were there when people weren’t.
  • They bring a sense of luck and emotional comfort.
  • They connect me to anime and Japanese culture.
